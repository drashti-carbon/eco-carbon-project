About CarbonManager

CarbonManager is an interactive tool designed to calculate and track your personal carbon footprint. By answering questions about your daily lifestyle, transportation, energy use, water consumption, and plastic usage, you get instant feedback on how your actions impact the environment.

This tool also provides personalized suggestions and an eco-plan to help you reduce your carbon footprint and become more environmentally responsible. Our goal is to raise awareness and encourage small, daily changes that collectively make a big difference for our planet.
