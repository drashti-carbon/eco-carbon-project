Future Scope

Enhancing CarbonManager

Our vision for CarbonManager includes expanding its capabilities with modern technology to further reduce carbon footprints and promote sustainability:

  • AI-based carbon prediction: Use machine learning to predict future carbon emissions based on your lifestyle and suggest optimal habits.
  • Integration with smart meters: Monitor electricity, water, and gas usage in real-time to provide accurate feedback.
  • Mobile app version: Access the calculator anywhere and track progress daily.
  • Gamification: Encourage users with rewards for reducing their carbon footprint over time.

These upgrades will make CarbonManager more engaging, precise, and impactful for sustainable living.
